How to make noodles:-

1-Take a vessel and pour water in it.
2-Boil the water for few minutes
3-Add the the noodles inside the Vessel/Bowl
4-Boil it for a few minute's
5-Add your favorite masala
6-Close the bowl with a lid and let it boil for a few minutes
7-After that take a bowl and a fork and add the cooked noodles in it
8-Enjoy your Noodles
